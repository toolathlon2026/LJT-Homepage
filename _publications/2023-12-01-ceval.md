Evaluating foundation models in Chinese remains challenging due to the lack of comprehensive benchmarks. C‑Eval fills this gap by providing a multi‑level, multi‑discipline evaluation suite that spans from elementary to professional levels across STEM, humanities, and social sciences. The dataset comprises over 10,000 high‑quality questions, each carefully verified by domain experts. We evaluate a wide range of foundation models on C‑Eval and analyze their performance across disciplines and difficulty levels. The results reveal significant room for improvement, especially in advanced scientific and professional subjects. C‑Eval serves as a robust benchmark for tracking progress in Chinese language understanding and reasoning.
